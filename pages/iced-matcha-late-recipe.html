Iced Matcha Late

Recipe submitted by justonecookbook


Iced Matcha Latte takes just minutes to make but will keep you energized all day long. This creamy and refreshing green tea powder drink is packed with antioxidants, too. Learn how to make this ice-cold beverage at home that tastes better than Starbucks.

Ingredients Needed:

  • Matcha
  • Hot water (about 176ÂºF)
  • Milk
  • simple syrup

The Cooking Steps:

  1. Add ice cubes, milk, and simple syrup to a glass cup.
  2. Whisk matcha and hot water until no lumps remain. The matcha should be smooth and a little foamy.
  3. Assemble: Pour the matcha mixture into the milk glass. Enjoy!
